Hello!


My name is Eric. I am a software engineer and front-end developer based in the Chicagoland area. I have previously worked for four years at Danico Enterprises as a Junior Web Developer, and I currently work at Motorola Solutions as a UI/UX Engineer.

Additionally, I made the Dean's List for five semesters and won the Computer Science Department's end-of-semester competition for my desktop security application SafePort (information about this project can be found here).


Throughout my years working in the tech field, I have discovered my strong passions for software engineering, psychology, and design, which I believe are the three cornerstones to front-end development. This is why I study and practice these subjects in my free time, including graphic design as one of my hobbies. When I'm not doing career-related activities, I am typically watching horror movies, attending rock and metal concerts, golfing, longboarding, appreciating nature, or working on other fun, creative personal projects.


At work, I have been frequently commended on my organization, efficiency, and keen eye for design. Some of my additional strengths are honesty, integrity, and communication, and some of the most important tools I am proficient in are HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, Node, Joomla, Python, and Photoshop.
