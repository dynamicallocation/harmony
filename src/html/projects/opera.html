Project Encore


Opera is a powerful internet browser that is offered on both desktop and mobile platforms, and in my opinion, its desktop version is the best out of any of the popular modern browsers. Opera Touch is the mobile version of Opera, and while it is also quite a powerful app, its UI and especially UX are lacking.


Project Encore was my semester project for my Human-Computer Interaction class during my fourth year at Loyola University. The link to its repository can be found here. The goal of Project Encore was to redesign the Opera Touch mobile browser to elevate it to the same level as its desktop counterpart. Development steps for this project included research, paper mockups, electronic mockups and prototyping in Adobe XD, and conducting user and accessibility testing.


Throughout this project, I greatly enhanced my UI/UX skills and dove deeply into important design concepts like typography, consistency, and mental models. Overall, the prospect of re-imagining the Opera Touch mobile app turned out to be a fantastic success.
